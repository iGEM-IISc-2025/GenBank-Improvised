GenBank-Improvised

An intuitive interface for the NCBI nucleotide database, with improved searching and visualizations.

Welcome to GenBank-Improvised, an enhanced platform built on the foundation of GenBank, one of the world’s most widely used repositories for genetic sequence data. GenBank, maintained by the National Center for Biotechnology Information (NCBI), hosts DNA, RNA, and protein sequences submitted by scientists worldwide and is foundational to research in molecular biology, medicine, biotechnology, and evolutionary studies.

While GenBank provides a wealth of information, navigating and interpreting its vast resources can be challenging. GenBank-Improvised was created to make this data more accessible, intuitive, and researcher-friendly, helping students, educators, and scientists engage with genetic information more efficiently.

Why GenBank-Improvised?

Everything you need: search, view plasmids, and documentation — all in one clean, powerful interface.

Search Effortlessly

Instantly find and browse genetic sequences with a modern search experience built on GenBank's API, with an imrpoved UI to make it easier to navigate.

Visualize Plasmids

See plasmids directly in your browser. No need to switch between tools, browse, analyze, and visualize genetic constructs in one place.

Clear Documentation

Everything is properly documented from search options to visualization tools. No more guessing parameters buried in PDFs.
